Problem Space

The college application process can be stressful and confusing for high school students. It leads to life-changing decisions. Due to all the stress, student may have trouble organizaing their applications if applying to several colleges or universities. Each application has it's own requirements, essays, recommendation letters, and more. Students may miss deadlines due to the lengthy process or the expensive fees for applications. Not all students receive the assistance they need in completing applications.

These are the problems we wish to address with our website!

What's Offered

Home

The homepage offers quicklinks to get in contact with your counselor as well as interesting new articles pertaining to the college world.

Applications

All of your college applications on one page! Keep track of your college applications by checking your progress and editing the application. Avoid confusion and check the application requirements and fee information for any of the colleges you're applying to.

Colleges

Still unsure of which college to apply to? Search for a college or university! Whether you want Illinois schools or out of state schools, searching will bring you to different school websites so that you can learn more about them.

Calendar

Calendar allows you to keep all your deadlines and events all in one place. Add events or reminders to avoid forgetting to ask for that recommendation letter or to submit that essay.

Chat

Keep in contact with your conselor or teacher with the chat! Let them know you're struggling with that essay prompt and schedule an appointment. View your counselors office hours and schedule an appointment. Need transcripts or fee waivers? Send them a request!

Resources

Avoid confusion by checking the resources page. Find information from various college topics randing from financial aid to ACt and SAT study tips! The resources page offers helpful links to scholarship websites as well as how to pick the right college or college major for you.
